Our Classes

Our training sessions are open to all skill and cardio levels from beginner to advanced. Training sessions consist of a combination of drills, pad-work, and for more advanced members - clinching and sparring.
The classes are designed to improve your cardio and striking ability.
Any required gear is available at the gym for those who don't have their own.

Kids Classes

The kids classes teaches kids the fundamentals of the sport, while also mixing in fun games. This shows the kids the basic principals of muay thai, while keeping things light-hearted and playful. The aim of these classes is to get the kids interested in the sport, while ensuring they have fun and enjoy each session.
